Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI tutoring work?

Our AI analyzes your learning materials and questions, then provides personalized explanations, step-by-step solutions, and interactive learning experiences tailored to your specific needs and learning style.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes to your plan will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What subjects does Visio Tutor cover?

We cover a wide range of subjects including mathematics, sciences, languages, humanities, computer science, and more. The free plan includes basic coverage, while paid plans offer comprehensive support across all subjects.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, Visio Tutor is available on iOS and Android devices. You can download our app from the App Store or Google Play Store to learn on the go.

How secure is my data?

We take data privacy seriously. All your personal information and learning materials are encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
